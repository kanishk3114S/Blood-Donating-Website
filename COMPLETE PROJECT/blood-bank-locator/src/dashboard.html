Health & Diet Advice for Donors

Eat iron-rich foods like spinach, legumes, and fish before and after donating blood.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can donate blood?
Healthy adults between 18 and 65 years, weighing more than 50kg, and not on prohibited medications can generally donate. Always check local guidelines.
What are the steps for donating blood?
1. Register for donation
2. Complete a basic health screening
3. Donate blood (15 min process)
4. Rest and have refreshments afterward.
What care should I take after donating?
Drink extra water, avoid intense exercise for 24 hours, enjoy a healthy meal, and keep the bandage on for several hours.
